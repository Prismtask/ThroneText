Throne of Plaintext
A rich, story-driven text-based dungeon crawler RPG

Overview
Throne of Plaintext is a deep, classic-style text RPG where you explore dangerous dungeons, manage a growing adventurer, build relationships with cities, and face increasingly powerful foes — including epic superbosses that change the battlefield with unique mechanics.
You start as a fresh adventurer in the city of Solmere and gradually unlock more cities, better gear, houses, monster girl companions, and powerful abilities. Time passes realistically, affecting difficulty, shop stock, and bounty deadlines. Every choice matters.
Core Loop

Visit Cities — Rest, shop, train, upgrade your house, take bounties, or buy ships.
Descend into Dungeons — Clear floors filled with enemies and bosses. Each city has its own themed dungeon.
Grow Stronger — Level up, craft/enhance gear, collect rare items, and capture monster girls.
Travel the World — Journey between cities by land or sea. Random encounters and events keep every trip exciting.

Key Features

Character Creation — Choose from multiple races and classes with meaningful attribute bonuses.
Deep Progression — Attributes, equipment enhancement, scrolls, blessings, training buffs, and house upgrades.
Living World — Multiple interconnected cities, each with unique services, dialogue, and dungeon biome.
Time & Difficulty System — Day/night cycle and time of day affect enemy strength and travel danger.
Epic Superbosses — Every 10th floor brings a unique, mechanically rich superboss fight (Broodmother, Slitcurrent, Sylvana, Ignis, Yinglong, etc.).
Monster Girl System — Capture compatible monster girls and raise affection in your houses.
Bounties — Dynamic, scaling hunt contracts with deadlines and rewards.
Inventory & Economy — Buy, sell, enhance, and fuse items. Black markets offer rare goods.
Travel Events — Random encounters, merchants, hazards, and sea storms on journeys.
Save System — Multiple save slots with per-city dungeon progress tracking.

How to Play

Run the game:Bashpython main.py
Create a new character or load an existing save.
In cities, choose services from the menu:
Shop, Inn, Blacksmith, Temple, Guild (bounties), House, etc.
Travel to other cities (land or sea).

When ready, enter the local dungeon and descend floor by floor.
Survive, grow stronger, and uncover the secrets of the world.

Controls

Most interactions use numbered choices.
In combat: Attack, Defend, Flee, Use item, Capture (when available), Wield the Abyss (when you have the legendary weapon).
[I]nventory and [S]ave are available after most room victories.

Technical Info

Pure Python (no external dependencies).
Saves are stored in the savefile/ folder.
Modular design — easy to extend cities, enemies, items, or superbosses.
